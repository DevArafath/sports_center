The Ritz Club London

Luxury snooker lounge installation with custom mahogany tables and premium felt.

The Ritz Club London

Project Details

Client: The Ritz Club

Location: London, UK

Completion: March 2023

Project Type: Commercial

Size: 2,500 sq ft

Duration: 3 months

Project Overview

This prestigious project for The Ritz Club London involved the complete renovation of their exclusive members-only snooker lounge. We installed four custom mahogany tournament-grade snooker tables with premium Strachan 6811 cloth and solid brass fittings.

The comprehensive project also included:

  • Custom ambient lighting with dimmable LED fixtures
  • Handcrafted spectator seating with premium leather upholstery
  • Bespoke cue racks and accessories storage
  • Complete interior design with acoustic treatments
  • Professional-grade scoring systems

Working closely with the club's management, we delivered a luxurious space that honors the traditional elegance of snooker while incorporating modern comforts and technology. The project was completed ahead of schedule and has received acclaim from club members and professional players alike.
