About Us

Established in 2005, Crown Billiards International is a leading provider of premium pool and snooker solutions. We specialize in the supply and installation of our exclusive “Crown Billiards International” pool tables, renowned for their quality craftsmanship and superior performance.

In addition to our signature pool tables, we offer a comprehensive range of high-end, premium, and standard accessories for both pool and snooker. From professional cues to essential table fittings, we deliver everything needed to create the perfect playing environment. With a commitment to excellence and over two decades of industry expertise, we provide end-to-end solutions—from product supply to full-scale installation—ensuring a seamless experience for pool halls, clubs, and private gaming spaces.


At Crown Billiards International, we don't just sell products—we deliver quality, precision, and a passion for the game.
